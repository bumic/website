ABOUT

Boston University Machine Intelligence Community is a space for sharing and discussing machine learning research, articles, ideas, and current events. We are also officially a student-led research group sponsored by Boston University's Rafik B. Hariri Institute for Computing and Computational Science and Engineering, BU Software & Application Innovation Lab (SAIL), and BU Spark.
